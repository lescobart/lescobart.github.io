About Lucho

Colombian Brewed, Miami Infused

My name is Luis G Escobar, but since my dad has the same name, everyone calls me Lucho. I was born in Colombia, in 1991, and lived there until 2005 when I moved from Bogota to Miami, Fl. I went to Coral Gables Sr High School and Graduated with an APID and went on with my education at Miami Dade College - Wolfson Campus. I graduated MDC with an Associate Degree and went on to Florida International University to pursue my Bachelors in Art History. After a reality check in my life, I realized that I was meant to create and design graphics. In December of 2014 my daughter Alice Marie was born and my life changed, I started studying at Miami International University of Art and Design
